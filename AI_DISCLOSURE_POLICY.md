# AI Disclosure Policy

AI-assisted contributions are welcome, but must be labelled. Disclose the tool or model used where known, whether it generated code, analysis, text, diagrams, or search queries, whether outputs were independently checked, which claims remain unverified, and whether the contribution touches market-sensitive, operational, or regulated material. AI-generated claims are not accepted as evidence unless supported by sources, code, reproducible artefacts, or human review.

# Open Energy Modelling Tool Tracker

## Summary

Open Energy Transition's openmod-tracker collects open energy system modelling
tools from several upstream inventories and publishes repository, package,
documentation, and community-activity metrics through a dashboard.

## Fit To Problem

This contribution is linked to `EP-902` because it is a tool-level reference
for open energy modelling software health and adoption metrics.

It is complementary to this register. openmod-tracker is tool-first: it helps
answer which open modelling tools exist and how active or discoverable they
are. Open Energy Problems is problem-first: it asks which open references help
answer a particular grid, market, data, forecasting, or flexibility problem.

## Caveats

- Tool activity metrics do not prove that a tool solves a specific energy
  problem.
- The tracker focuses on energy system modelling tools, not all open energy
  datasets, registries, market disclosures, benchmarks, or AI-facing
  interfaces.
- Coverage of concrete geography-specific model instances, including German
  grid models, should be checked against the tracker inventory and upstream
  source lists.
